Why Opt for Open-source?

It’s an open-source world. About 78% of companies prefer open-source solutions. In addition to costs and independence from software vendors, competitive features and technical capabilities are the major spotlights of open-source development.

Information Security

Commercial open source has a solid information security record than proprietary software. Identifying and fixing problems in open-source is much easier than the code moulder in a proprietary environment.

Flexibility and Agility

Open-source enables technology agility, offers multiple ways to solve problems. Moreover, open-source is less resource-intensive, which means, you can run it well even on older hardware. And the best part, it’s totally up to you to decide when it's time to upgrade, not some vendor.

Cost-effectiveness

Open-source is typically much cost-effective than a proprietary solution, but that’s not all of it, it also gives enterprises the ability to start small and scale. For the enterprises that are budget-challenged, it makes financial sense to choose open source solutions.
